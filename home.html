Learn How to Play

Da Controls

In this game your mouse will serve as the controller.
With your mouse you can either 'left' or 'right' click.
Your 'left click' will select a squares without mines.
Your 'right click' will place a flags on mines.

Da Rules

Click squares and avoid mines.
Some squares will clear away on their own if no mine is in the immediate area.
Other squares will tell you how many mines are nearby. You can get a number between 1 & 4 telling you how many bombs its touching.
If you hit a mine you lose.

Good Luck!
